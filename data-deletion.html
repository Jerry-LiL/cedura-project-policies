Data Deletion

Cedura Project

Last updated: April 30, 2026

At the Cedura Project, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request the deletion of your personal data from our systems, what data is covered, and what to expect after you submit a request.

1. Your Right to Deletion

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR, CCPA, and similar regulations), you have the right to request that we delete the personal data we hold about you. We will honour your request unless we are required or permitted to retain certain data by law, regulation, or for legitimate business purposes (such as fraud prevention or dispute resolution).

2. What Data Can Be Deleted

Upon a verified deletion request, we will delete or anonymise:

  • Your account profile information (name, email address, username).
  • Activity and usage logs associated with your account.
  • Preferences and settings stored under your account.
  • Any content you have posted or submitted, unless retaining it is necessary for legal or safety reasons.
  • Communications and support tickets linked to your account.

3. Data We May Retain

In some circumstances we are required or permitted to retain certain data even after a deletion request. This includes:

  • Records required to comply with applicable legal obligations.
  • Transaction records needed for financial reporting or tax purposes.
  • Data necessary to resolve ongoing disputes or enforce our agreements.
  • Aggregated and anonymised data that can no longer be linked back to you.
Note: Deleting your data is irreversible. Once your account and associated data are deleted, they cannot be recovered.

4. How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Log in to your account and navigate to Settings > Privacy > Delete My Data, then follow the on-screen instructions; or
  2. Send an email to privacy@cedura-project.example with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Please include the email address associated with your account so we can identify and verify your identity.

To protect your privacy and security, we may ask you to verify your identity before processing the deletion request. We will not delete data based on unverified requests.

5. What Happens After You Submit a Request

  • We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days.
  • Your data will be deleted or anonymised within 30 days of verification, unless a legal exception applies.
  • If we need additional time (up to a further 60 days for complex requests), we will notify you of the extension and the reason.
  • Once the deletion is complete, we will send you a confirmation.

6. Third-Party Data

If you signed up or logged in using a third-party service (such as Google, Facebook, or another OAuth provider), you should also revoke Cedura Project's access within that third-party service's settings. We only store the data provided to us by those services and will delete our copy upon request; however, the third-party retains its own records independently.

7. Withdrawing from Marketing Communications

If you only want to stop receiving marketing or promotional emails without deleting your account and data entirely, you can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any marketing email, or by contacting us at privacy@cedura-project.example.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about data deletion or your privacy rights, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@cedura-project.example

For more information about how we handle your data, please review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
